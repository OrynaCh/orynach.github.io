About us

"Health Patrons" is a young initiative founded by three Kyiv-Mohyla Academy graduates: Oryna Chubych, Svitlana Myrilenko and Viktoria Kuchirka. The purpose of this initiative is to help actual people who become injured in the course of the war. We connect Ukrainian men and women who were injured during the Russian invasion of Ukraine with families in Europe and the USA - "patrons" - who are ready to support treatment or rehabilitation financially.

How does it work?

Wounded people who need help and Patrons who are ready to provide such help contact us. We help these people to get in touch with each other for first-hand, personal assistance. In this way, patrons help the wounded directly and can be sure that they are supporting the treatment and rehabilitation of real people who need their help the most.

Become a patron

Right away you can help one specific person who needs you. We do not act as an intermediary, but will connect you directly with injured people who need financial support during treatment and rehabilitation. Sometimes the injured person does not have the means to receive funds and report on their use independently. In such case the patron can transfer the funds directly to the hospital. We will make sure that all the money goes to the treatment of this particular injured person, and we as organization will provide a spending report to the patron.

Even a small amount can significantly improve treatment conditions, speed up the recovery and return to the normal life.

You can help:

  • with a one-time payment. Such help is especially needed for the initial treatment stages: to cover the diagnostics, surgical intervention, etc.
  • with regular financial support. Many need rehabilitation and your regular support will help the injured to return to life step by step,
  • with consultation and physical support for the wounded. Some of the wounded are undergoing rehabilitation in EU/UK/USA, where they need help from the locals - help with documents, translation, and provision of basic needs. Such help is also very valuable.

Need help?

  • you or your relatives suffered as a result of Russian aggression, you are currently in Ukraine and you need financial assistance during treatment or rehabilitation
  • you have a wartime injury and currently are being threated abroad and need any help
